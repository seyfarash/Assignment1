Welcome to my online portfolio! The objective of this is to try and showcase all of my wonderful creations that I have made over the years as a Computer Sciences and Computer Programming Analyst student. The reason that I chose to become a computer sciences student is mainly due to the fact that I grew up around people who worked in the computer field my entire life. I hope to someday find a career path in a field of computer sciences onme day (as most of us dream, somewhere in game design hopefully !).
